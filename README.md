# [Pokemon-UNITE-Random-Selector](https://jihunkimcode.github.io/Pokemon-UNITE-Random-Selector/)
A simple tool to randomly choose a Pokémon from Pokémon UNITE.

## How to Use
1. **Pick a Pokémon**
   - Click **“Pick a Pokémon”** to select a Pokémon randomly.
   - Use the **Volume Panel** to control the Pokémon's cry.
2. **Sorting**
   - Sort Pokémon **A–Z** or by **release date**.
3. **Filtering**
   - Click on an individual Pokémon to **enable or disable** it from the selection pool.
   - Use the **position buttons** to enable or disable Pokémon by role (Attacker, Defender, etc.).
4. **Save & Load**
   - Click **Save State** to save all currently enabled Pokémon.
   - Click **Load State** to restore them later.

## Important
* All images and data are sourced from the [Pokémon UNITE Official Website](https://unite.pokemon.com/en-us/).
* Sounds are sourced from the [PokéAPI](https://pokeapi.co/).
* Available Pokémons are kept up to date based on the official Pokémon UNITE listings.
